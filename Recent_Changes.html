Change Date: 11/10/2021

Updated Intermediate Bond Return rates to match the SIMBA spreadsheet. These rates are lower than the previous version.

 

Change Date: 01/01/2022

Added new feature to allow naming and maintaining multiple plans.

Added new feature to encourage filling a lower tax bracket when anticipating higher tax brackets in the future.

Added new feature to allow defining when your want to start drawing from your Roth.

Updated tax brackets and Medb premium rates for 2022.

Allow definition of baseline inflation rate.

Allow definition of asset allocation by GoGo, NoGo, SloGo and assisted living phases of retirement.

Minor report format changes.

 

Change Date: 02/01/2022

Added new feature to account for qualified charitable distribution deductions (QCD) on income tax calculation.

Change Date: January 2023

Updated summary report to limit the number of failures shown. Cleaned up some of the formatting.

Allowed setting social security start age to 71. For people whose birthday are in the second half of the year, this is a more conservative value to use for the simulation purposes.

Added option to include home equity into the simulation. Home equity will be tapped as the last option if the simulation runs out of money in all other accounts first.

Separated out Roth and H.S.A. balances into separate accounts. How and when money is used in each of these accounts is now treated differently by RPS.

Added option to allow RPS to calculate your estimated MedB premiums. Do not include MedB premiums in your annual budget if you decide to let RPS calculate estimated MedB premiums each year simulated.

Added flag to use H.S.A funds for MedB premiums.

Allow for a flat draw amount of H.S.A funds. You may have saved money in H.S.A. account and “shoe boxed” the medical claims and receipts to be used later to draw on our H.S.A. account. This will allow you to define and plan for using those unreimbursed claim funds from H.S.A. funds during retirement.

Added option to reduce the amount of data initially reported on the detail report.

Updated tax tables and MedB premium tables for 2023 rates.

Updated inflation rate, stock market performance, and bond fund performance for 2022.
